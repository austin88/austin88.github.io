Down at an English fair,
one evening I was there

Im not always at the fair, you see.

When I heard a showman shouting underneath the flair

Assuming Im already there, I can not miss him.

I have got a lovely bunch of coconuts

They are not that great.

There they are, all standing in a row

It is true that he lined them up quite nicely.

Big ones, small ones, some as big as your head

They are of variable sizes, after all.

Give them a twist, a flick of the wrist,
that is what the showman said

I will only play when I feel like doing so.
